Worlds eSports

Event Based Packaging

Worlds 2022 eSports is competition hosted by Riot Games, for their game League of Legends. For this event Riot has launched a limited edition Worlds apparel line.

Project Overview

Develop an event-based package design for Riot Games Worlds apparel for their Worlds 2022 event, and develop a package design solution that effectively captures the essence of the brand and event.

Challenge

How may I effectively promote the Worlds event, while maintaining the brands current identity and create an innovative package design solution that is impactful, memorable, and inspire customer loyalty and interaction.

Final Packaging

Rationale

My packaging for the World’s e-sports event by Riot Games is a capsule that twists open to reveal the limited World’s apparel inside with a redeemable thank you card for players. The package itself is driven by the concept of bringing the gaming experience to real life and the fact that the products are collectable limited edition merchandise — I wanted to extend the collectable aspect into the packaging itself. 

The capsule itself is a collectable display piece alone or can be reused to house any League collectable figurine.

Process

Key Concept

My driving concept for this project was how could I bring gaming to real life and bring out in-game experiences to the package and event itself.

The package should be intuitive and easy to use and mimic the feeling of opening in-game rewards. Invoking feelings of a winner, surprise, memorable, excitement, wonder, and enjoyment.

Inspiration

In-game there is an event capsule for the Worlds eSports event, like the regular capsules players can play and earn a capsule by playing and open them for in game rewards (skins, in-game currency, champions, etc). To stay true to my concept I felt it was only fitting I referred to an in-game item which could not only hold the gift item but be re-used as a display piece as is or hold future league purchases such as figurines of their favourite champion.

Sketches

Because the product is a limited edition exclusive apparel line for the event I wanted to make the packaging reflect that prestigious status. While also tying it back to the game League of Legends inspired by my concept “Bringing gaming to life”.

Furthermore, I wanted to bring elements and aspects of something the player base would love into the real world during an event they have decided to log off and attend in real life.

Vinyl Wrap Illustration

The outside barrel of the capsule is vinyl wrapped with an illustration of Infernal drake — an objective from the game.

The nature of dragons being possessive creatures who protect expensive and important items added to the exclusivity and prestige of the apparel line. Put across the packaging as if it were guarding the contents inside.

Visual Design

In order to match the feel of Worlds I used the branding documented on Riot Games and referenced Worlds media and content.

Riot Games, Worlds branding utilizes elements and assets that are vibrant and reflective of eSports — vibrant, lively, energetic, digital, and sporty.

Creation

Prototyping

My first prototype towards the capsule was a folded paper mock up. With this mockup I was first thinking about the shape and form, really thinking of how I can relate it to the game itself.

Modeling

After some more sketching and discussing the mechanics of it I chose to have it modeled and 3D print my packaging in order to ensure the base and mechanics all work properly. From there I would paint and add detailing.

3D Printing

By this time I was able to get a physical print and finally start working on detailing. From here I began sanding it down to give it a nice and smooth finish and get it prepped for spray painting.

Painting

Once I had fully sanded down each piece till it was smooth to the touch and blemish free I applied the first coat of white paint. Followed up with gold detailing, and lastly spraying on the blue to the top and bottom.
